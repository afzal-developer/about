Started My Career

At that time, I was proficient in developing websites with WordPress & Elementor. I used to design websites with page builders like divi, elementor... customizing different themes... Woocommerce web-stores. In short - I was a WordPress Developer at that time.

2023

HTML & CSS

This is was the time when I stepped forward and started developing websites with HTML & CSS. To expand my skills. Finally, I got a title of Coder as well. Before that I was just a WordPress Developer. Bringing HTML & CSS in my skills made me able to develop any kind of custom website with functionalities desired by the clients.

2025

Full Stack Developer

As my journey progressed, I didn’t stop at just front-end development. I took the leap into becoming a Full Stack Developer, mastering both the front-end and back-end realms of web development. This transition allowed me to build dynamic, scalable, and fully functional web applications from scratch. I expanded my skill set to include JavaScript, Node.js, React, and databases like MySQL and MongoDB. Now, I could not only design visually appealing interfaces with HTML, CSS, and modern frameworks but also handle server-side logic, APIs, and database management. This evolution from a WordPress Developer to a Coder, and finally to a Full Stack Developer, reflects my commitment to growth and my passion for creating seamless, end-to-end digital experiences. Each step of my journey has been about pushing boundaries, learning new technologies, and delivering solutions that exceed client expectations.
